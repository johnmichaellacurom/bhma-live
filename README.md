# Boarding House Mobile Application
- This application is designed to help boarding house owners efficiently manage their properties and daily operation. It provides a simple yet powerful system for organizing rooms, tracking boarders, monitoring payments, and maintaining accurate records.

# Formula for Electric Bill
## Total Bill is less than Transient Discount
- Total Bill: 50
- Transient Discount: 60
- 
- Boarder 1 belongings: 50
- Boarder 2 belongings: 120
- 
- Belongings: 50 + 120 = 170
- 
- Boarder 1: 50 / 170 * 50 = 14.71
- Boarder 2: 120 / 170 * 50 = 35.29
- 
- DiscountRatio: 50 / 60 = 0.8333
- 
- Final Boarder 1: 14.71 * (1 - 0.8333) = 2.45
- Final Boarder 2: 35.29 * (1 - 0.8333) = 5.88
- 
- Boarder Belongings Amount to Pay
- 1        50           2.45
- 2        120          5.88
## Total Bill is greater than Transient Discount
- Total Bill: 150
- Transient Discount: 60
- 
- Boarder 1 belongings: 50
- Boarder 2 belongings: 120
- 
- Belongings: 50 + 120 = 170
- 
- Boarder 1: 50 / 170 * 150 = 44.12
- Boarder 2: 120 / 170 * 150 = 105.88
- 
- DiscountRatio: 60 / 150 = 0.4
- 
- Final Boarder 1: 44.12 * (1 - 0.4) = 26.47
- Final Boarder 2: 105.88 * (1 - 0.4) = 63.53
- 
- Boarder Belongings Amount to Pay
- 1        50           26.47

# Formula for Water Bill

- Transient Discount: 60

-

- Boarder 2: 50 / 2 = 25
-
- DiscountRatio: 50 / 60 = 0.8333
